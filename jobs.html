Green Careers

Agricultural Worker

If gardening and outdoor work has alwyas been your stress relief, why not turn your passion into something profitable? Now that many food items on groceries are posioned with chemicals, sustainable farming is quicky becoming a global trend. By pursuing this career, ypu can make sure the food you bring home is naturally grown.

Recycler

From enormous piles of paper to mountains of plastic, waste accumulation is showing no signs of slowing down. Fortunately, you could take a proactive step in fighting this environmental problem by pursuing a career as a recycler. The job is actually pretty simple, mostly you just have to sort materials and isolate hazardous ones.

Plumbers, Steamfitters, and Pipefitters

If you're a plumber, steamfitter, or a pipefitter, you don't need to limit your potential to servicing households. It's even bigger than that. In various gren sectors, the installation and maintenance of geothermal, solar, and ethanol power plants necessitate your specific skill sets.

Carpenters

As more industries are becoming increasingly eco-conscious, the need for eco-friendly housing and buildings are on the rise. And as we know, all types of construction require carpentry. Typically, green carpentry involves the use of recycled materials that have less environmental impacts. Aside from echo-friendly housing, many urban houses are also adding greenhouses for their gardening needs.

Green Electricians

Green or not, a technological device is made up of circuits of wires that conduct electricity. Thanks to the need for eco-friendly electrical sources, the green industry is now on the lookout for skilled electricians. You could become a pro at dismanting solar panels and straightening out wiring systems.

Park Ranger

Park and forest rangers play an important role in conserving the environment. As the job implies, they mainly preserve and protect parks or forests from any potential harm. They can work in an amazing range and variety of environments from urban historical sites to remote mountain posts.

Environmental Technician

Environmental Technicians look for sources of pollution and anything that could contaminate the envirnonment or negatively affect public health. To do this, they collect samples and perform various tests.

Nuclear Engineer

Nuclear Engineers develop, monitor and operate nuclear plants that produce power. They ensure these plants are functioning and that the design, construction and overall operational practices meet necessary safety regulations.
